REQUEST FOR DONATION FOR SPORTS

Empower 500 Tribal Youth Through a State-of-the-Art Sports Complex at KASH Campus, Golanthara

At Karuna Shanti (KASH) +2 Science and Vocational Training Campus in Golanthara, Berhampur, Odisha, we are committed to the holistic development of Scheduled Tribe (ST), Scheduled Caste (SC), and OBC boys and girls. Recognizing the transformative power of sports in fostering discipline, teamwork, and physical well-being, we propose the construction of a comprehensive Sports Complex to serve our 500 students.

Project Overview:

  • 200m Synthetic Running Track: Encircling the central field, suitable for athletics training and competitions.
  • Central Football and Cricket Field: A versatile turf accommodating both sports.
  • Basketball Court: FIBA-standard synthetic surface.
  • Badminton Court: Synthetic flooring suitable for indoor or covered play.
  • Volleyball Court: Outdoor court with appropriate surfacing.
  • Additional Amenities: Seating areas, lighting, and basic equipment storage facilities.

Estimated Budget:

  • 200m Synthetic Running Track: ₹6–7 Crores
  • Football/Cricket Field: ₹1–1.5 Crores
  • Basketball Court: ₹15–20 Lakhs
  • Badminton Court: ₹20–25 Lakhs
  • Volleyball Court: ₹10–15 Lakhs
  • Additional Amenities: ₹50 Lakhs
  • Total Estimated Cost: Approximately ₹8–9 Crores

Impact:

  • Promote Physical Health: Encourage regular physical activity among students.
  • Foster Talent: Provide a platform for identifying and nurturing sports talent.
  • Enhance Discipline and Teamwork: Instill values of discipline, teamwork, and perseverance.
  • Boost Academic Performance: Physical well-being contributes to better focus and academic success.

By supporting this initiative, you play a pivotal role in transforming the lives of these young individuals, offering them a safe and supportive environment to pursue their dreams.

Donate today and be a part of building a brighter future.
