REQUEST FOR DONATION FOR LIBRARY

Help Build a Dedicated Library for Marginalized Society Youth in Golanthara, Odisha.

At Karuna Shanti (KASH) +2 Science and Vocational Training Campus in Golanthara, Berhampur, we are committed to empowering ST, SC, and OBC boys and girls through quality education and skill-building. Our students — many of whom come from remote tribal and underprivileged backgrounds — have immense potential but lack access to even the most basic academic resources.

One of our most urgent needs is a dedicated library with a separate reading area for boys and girls. Currently, students struggle with limited books, no quiet space to study, and no exposure to reference materials or digital learning tools.

With your support, we aim to build a state-of-the-art library that will:

  • Offer a rich collection of textbooks, science journals, career guides, and digital resources.
  • Create safe, gender-sensitive reading zones, encouraging focus and participation, especially for girls.
  • Inspire independent learning, critical thinking, and academic excellence.
  • Serve as a hub for evening study, mentorship, and creative expression.

A well-equipped library is not just a building — it is a gateway to opportunity, especially for first-generation learners. Your donation will help lay the foundation for hundreds of students to dream beyond their limits.

Join us in building minds that will build the future.

Contribute today — because books open doors no walls can hold shut.

(Donations are eligible for 80G tax benefits. Project details, budget, and impact reports are available on request.)
