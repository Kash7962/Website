EXTENSION OF COLLEGE BUILDING FOR MASS EDUCATION FOR CLASS 9 & 10TH

Building a Mass Education Extension for 1,000 Students at KASH Campus, Golanthara

At Karuna Shanti (KASH) +2 Science and Vocational Training Campus in Golanthara, Berhampur, Odisha, we are committed to providing quality education to Scheduled Tribe (ST), Scheduled Caste (SC), and OBC boys and girls. To accommodate the growing number of students and enhance our educational offerings, we propose the construction of a Mass Education Extension Building for classes 9 and 10, designed to seat 1,000 students.

Project Overview:

  • Land Availability: We have secured land for the construction of the extension building.
  • Construction Plan:
    • Total Built-up Area: Approximately 40,000 sq. ft., including classrooms, laboratories, administrative offices, and common areas.
    • Facilities: Modern classrooms equipped with smart boards, science and computer labs, library, staff rooms, and sanitation facilities.

Estimated Budget:

  • Construction Cost: ₹1,800 to ₹2,500 per sq. ft., depending on materials and finishes.
  • Total Estimated Cost: Between ₹6.6 crores to ₹8.2 crores.

Impact:

  • Expand Educational Access: Provide quality education to an additional 1,000 students from marginalized communities.
  • Enhance Learning Environment: Modern facilities will foster better academic performance and holistic development.
  • Promote Social Equity: Empower underprivileged students with opportunities for upward mobility.
