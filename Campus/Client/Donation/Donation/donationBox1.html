REQUEST FOR DONATION FOR CCTV CAMERA

Enhance Campus Safety with Smart Surveillance for Marginalized Students in Golanthara, Odisha

At Karuna Shanti (KASH) +2 Science and Vocational Training Campus in Golanthara, Berhampur, we are dedicated to empowering Scheduled Tribe (ST), Scheduled Caste (SC), and OBC boys and girls through quality education and vocational training. Ensuring a safe and secure environment is paramount for our students to thrive academically and personally.

To bolster campus security, we aim to install a state-of-the-art IP-based CCTV surveillance system with optical fiber connectivity across critical areas—classrooms, dormitories, laboratories, libraries, dining halls, and campus perimeters. This system will be monitored centrally from a dedicated security control room, enabling real-time surveillance and prompt response to any incidents.

Estimated Budget Breakdown:

  • High-definition IP Cameras: ₹2,500–₹5,000 per unit
  • Installation Charges: ₹950–₹4,500 per camera
  • Optical Fiber Cabling and Network Infrastructure: ₹1,000–₹2,000 per meter, depending on quality and installation complexity
  • Network Video Recorder (NVR) System: ₹15,000–₹25,000
  • Security Control Room Setup: ₹50,000–₹1,00,000

Note: The total cost will vary based on the number of cameras, the total area to be covered, and specific site requirements.

Implementing this advanced surveillance system will:

  • Ensure the safety and well-being of over 500 tribal and marginalized students.
  • Deter potential security threats, fostering a secure learning environment. Due to the proximity of the campus to a Forest reserve nearby, we have many wild animals wandering into the campus in search of food.
  • Facilitate efficient monitoring, allowing staff to respond swiftly to emergencies.
  • Comply with safety standards, enhancing the institution's credibility and trust among stakeholders.

Your generous contribution will directly impact the lives of these students, providing them with a secure environment to pursue their educational aspirations.
