After functioning for three years inside the city it has been shifted to the outskirts of Brahmapur (Near Golanthara station) to its own building. The Programme Project Campus has hostel facility as well as day-boarding facility. For day-boarding students transport facility, Veg-lunch and refreshment, reading room is available. It has its own sophisticated campus with canteen, playground, 24 hours power supply, stationery store, broadband internet facility etc.
